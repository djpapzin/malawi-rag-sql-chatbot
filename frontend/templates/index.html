Welcome to Dwizani!

I am Dwizani, a prototype AI chatbot that can answer questions about infrastructure projects. My name means 'what you should know' in Chichewa. For now I have access to data on projects being constructed in Malawi.

Project Information

Ask about specific project details, budgets, and general information.

Example: "What is the total budget for infrastructure projects in the proj_dashboard table?"

Location-based Queries

Find projects by region, district, or specific locations.

Example: "Show me all projects in Zomba district"

Status Updates

Check project progress and completion status.

Example: "List all completed projects in the Southern Region"

Budget Analysis

Explore project budgets, expenditures, and financial metrics.

Example: "What is the average project budget by district?"

Contractor Information

Find information about project contractors and their assignments.

Example: "Who are the contractors working on projects in Lilongwe?"

Timeline Queries

Discover project timelines, start dates, and completion schedules.

Example: "Which projects started in fiscal year 2024?"
